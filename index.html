Who we are?

The Egypt Media Forum is an annual and independent international platform for media development, based in Cairo. The EMF aims to advance the know-how and development of media culture and policies, and to support capacity building and qualified bodies. We work on developing the media work environment in Egypt and the MENA region. The Forum runs throughout the year as an international platform for development in the form of sustainable products that aim to develop media tools. These include a press training unit and a digital platform providing advanced knowledge and utilizing in-depth studies. The Egypt Media Forum is owned by 30N for Strategic Communication.

Noha El Nahass – Forum Director

Egypt Media Forum is an annual forum and international platform for media development and advancement, held in Cairo. It aims to support advanced knowledge in the field of media and its policies, as well as to build qualified capacities and professionals.
